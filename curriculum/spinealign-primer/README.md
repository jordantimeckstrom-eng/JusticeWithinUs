# SpineAlignTime Primer — 1 Week Module

## Learning Objectives
- Understand the seven alignment goals and pillars.
- Use the PhoenixFly interface to perform daily rituals.
- Participate in Time Auction and tribal governance.
- Create and verify a Triple-Entry receipt.

## Day 1 — Foundations
- **Duration:** 45 minutes
- **Activities:** Intro video, three micro-exercises, reality prompt journaling.
- **Assessment:** Submit one micro-win.

## Day 2 — Dawn Protocol and Scheduler
- **Duration:** 60 minutes
- **Activities:** Configure dawn scheduler, run a simulated dawn ceremony.
- **Assessment:** Schedule a dawn and show `INCREMENT_DAWN` in the ledger.

## Day 3 — Tribes and Governance
- **Duration:** 60 minutes
- **Activities:** Join a tribe, propose a project, vote.
- **Assessment:** Submit a project proposal.

## Day 4 — Phoenix Chain and Triple-Entry
- **Duration:** 75 minutes
- **Activities:** Create a ledger entry, compute `tripleEntryHash`, simulate anchoring.
- **Assessment:** Export an encrypted snapshot and verify it.

## Day 5 — Integration and Reflection
- **Duration:** 60 minutes
- **Activities:** Group ritual, Time Auction practice, course reflection.
- **Assessment:** Final quiz and rubric scoring.

## Rubric
- Participation — 30%
- Practical tasks — 50%
- Quiz — 20%
